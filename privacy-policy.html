Privacy Policy

  • Author: Foster
  • Published On: June 01, 2023

Introduction

At Foster, we are committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our services as a service-based tech company. We encourage you to read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our practices and your rights regarding your personal data.

Information We Collect

  1. 1. Personal Information: We may collect personal information, such as your name, email address, contact number, and other relevant details when you voluntarily provide it to us while using our services or interacting with our website.
  2. 2. Usage Data: We may collect non-personal information about your interaction with our website and services. This may include your IP address, browser type, operating system, device information, referring URL, pages visited, and other similar information.
  3. 3. Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, gather usage information, and personalize content. You have the option to manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

Use of Information

  1. 1. Provide and Improve Services: We use your personal information to provide and improve our services, respond to inquiries, personalize user experience, and develop new features and functionalities.
  2. 2. Communication: We may use your contact information to communicate with you regarding updates, announcements, promotional materials, or to respond to your inquiries.
  3. 3. Analytics and Insights: We may use non-personal information, such as usage data, to analyze trends, monitor the effectiveness of our services, and enhance the overall user experience.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

  1. 1. Third-Party Service Providers: We may engage trusted third-party service providers to assist us in delivering our services, analyze data, or perform other business functions. These providers have access to the necessary information for their assigned tasks and are obligated to maintain the confidentiality of your data.
  2. 2. Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of others.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please note that no method of data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal information held by us. If you wish to exercise these rights or have any questions or concerns regarding your personal data, please contact us using the information provided below.

Updates to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated Privacy Policy on our website or through other communication channels.
